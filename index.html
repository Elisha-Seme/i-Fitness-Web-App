About i-Fitness

The most common problem in a fitness journey, is inconsistency due to lack of sustained motivation, At i-Fitness help individuals solve that using our "Reverse Reward" concept. Users set daily fitness goals linked to financial deposits. Achieving goals earns back part of the deposit, while misses result in forfeiture. This incentivizes consistent exercise, integrating with popular apps and sensors for detailed tracking and personalized goals.
